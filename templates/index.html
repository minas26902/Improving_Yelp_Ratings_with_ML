Natural Language Processing & few Analysis on Yelp dataset

Why Yelp dataset?

Yelp is a local business directory service and review site with social networking features. It allows users to give ratings and review businesses. The review is usually short text consisting of few lines with about hundred words. Often, a review describes various dimensions about a business and the experience of user with respect to those dimensions.This big dataset is a subset of Yelp's businesses, reviews, and user.

Our Goals are:

To discover what's in a review? Is it positive or negative? what the last sentences in the review can tell you? how to predict the reviewers based on their previouse reviews.
